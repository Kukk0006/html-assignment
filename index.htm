About Me

I'm from Thornton, Colorado, and I have been a resident in Ontario for 8 years now. I've lived in Markham and Peterborough. I have been in Ottawa since the start of 2019. I don't plan on staying in Ottawa in the long term, but don't have any idea where I want to go next. I have 2 years at least to figure that out.

My hobbies include:

  • photography
  • photo editing
  • sewing
  • miniature painting
  • playing guitar
  • tablet weaving
  • foam craft
  • and exploring more crafty hobbies

I grew up with a creative family. My mom taught me to sew and explore historical costuming. While my dad did some historical reenactment through the SCA and he was in a many bands while I was growing up.
